<analysis>Analyse the user's request to build a criminal appeal case management application.

**original_problem_statement:** 
The user wants to create an application designed to assist with criminal appeals, specifically focusing on murder cases under NSW and Australian federal law.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
1.  **Document Management:** The app must be able to store, sort, and organize various documents like briefs, case notes, and public case information.
2.  **Timeline Generation:** It should automatically create and update a timeline of events based on the information provided.
3.  **Multi-File Upload:** Allow users to upload several documents at once.
4.  **User Authentication:** The application must have a user login system.
5.  **AI-Powered Legal Analysis:**
    *   **Grounds of Merit Identification:** The app needs to analyze the uploaded documents to identify potential grounds of merit for an appeal. It must thoroughly investigate criminal law related to murder, manslaughter, and high court appeals.
    *   **Cross-Referencing:** The identified grounds must be cross-referenced with the source material from the documents.
6.  **Reporting System:** The app must provide three types of reports:
    *   **Quick Report:** A brief summary of the case.
    *   **Full Report:** A detailed analysis covering the available information, its potential use, the grounds of merit it supports, and references to relevant sections of law.
    *   **Extensive Log Report:** A comprehensive log of the entire case.
7.  **Notes and Comments:** Include a feature for lawyers to add notes and comments.
8.  **Presentation View:** Information should be presentable to a barrister, clearly outlining the grounds of merit, supporting evidence, and legal references.
9.  **Exporting:** The ability to export reports to PDF.

**User's preferred language**: English

**what currently exists?**
A full-stack web application has been developed using React, FastAPI, and MongoDB. The application supports Google social login for user authentication. The core functionalities include case creation and management, document uploading (with text extraction for TXT, PDF, and DOCX), and a notes/comments section. The application leverages an AI (GPT-5.2) to automatically identify Grounds of Merit from document content, perform deep-dive investigations on these grounds, and generate three distinct types of case reports. The UI is structured with a dashboard for case overviews and a detailed case view with tabs for Documents, Timeline, Notes, Grounds of Merit, and Reports.
